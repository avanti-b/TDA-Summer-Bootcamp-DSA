The Evolution of Data Science

How numbers became a whole personality 😎

Back in the day, data was just dusty numbers. Now? It’s literally driving cars, recommending your next fav show, and deciding which ad follows you across the internet 👀.

From OG statisticians to today’s prompt engineers — data science got that glow-up. Here’s how it evolved and why it’s straight-up running the tech world.

📅 Timeline of the Data Glow-Up

Era Tech Buzz Big Mood
1950s–80s Stats + Mainframes Math heads only 🤓
1990s Databases & SQL Spreadsheet nation 📉
2000s Big Data & Hadoop Server fans go brr 🖥️💨
2010s Python + ML DS = career goals 💼🔥
Now AI + LLMs Data doing stunts 😤

Why care though? 🤔

  • Businesses avoid flops with better data moves 📊
  • AI gets smarter — thanks to cleaner datasets 💡
  • We're literally part of a data pipeline right now 💀
  • Also, it just looks good on your resume ✨
